ℹ️ About

Brew Coach v0.4.5

Physics-informed pour-over extraction model. Two-phase kinetics with d²-scaled Fickian diffusion (Moroney et al. 2015/2019; Wang & Lim 2021) and Arrhenius temperature correction (Ea = 16 kJ/mol).

What's new in v0.4.5

Core model refactor (peer-reviewed):
• τ_slow now scales with d² (Fickian diffusion), not linear d
• Temperature enters kinetics via Arrhenius, not additive offset
• D_eff = 1.016×10⁻¹⁰ m²/s (fitted to baseline)
• EUI renamed to "Brew Confidence Score" (heuristic, not measured uniformity)
• Predictions show ±σ uncertainty band
• MAX_EXTRACTABLE re-tagged as CONVENTION-SCA
• F_s re-tagged as GRINDER-SPECIFIC-DEFAULT
• Geometry bias re-tagged as EMPIRICAL-FIT-USER-SPECIFIC
• RETAINED_RATIO re-tagged as HEURISTIC-DEFAULT
• LRR measurement added to back-calculator
• New optional brew-log fields: pour height, pour rate, jet integrity, spent bed mass
• Frost et al. 2023 cited on SCA chart
• Pour-technique effects remain qualitative only (Proposal E accepted)

Calibration Protocol

Exp 1 — Baseline: 3× standard recipe. Compare model vs measured TDS. Adjust D_eff if offset > 0.10%.
Exp 2 — Grind sweep: 3 settings × 3 replicates. Validate TDS-vs-grind slope. Falsification: if measured slope differs from predicted by > ±0.015 %/click.
Exp 3 — Pour count: 3× continuous vs 3× pulse. |ΔTDS| > 0.05% = real effect.
Exp 4 — Pour pattern: 3× centre vs 3× spiral. Repeat on flat-bottom.
Exp 9 — Pour height: 3 heights × 3 replicates (Park et al. 2025 validation).
New: Weigh spent bed after each brew for LRR measurement.
