    About Brew Coach v0.4.3

    A physics-informed pour-over model using two-phase extraction kinetics (Moroney et al. 2015), geometry bias (Frost 2019), and EUI heuristics.

    Constants are evidence-tagged: [PEER] [DERIVED] [CONSENSUS] [HEURISTIC]

    Pour-technique effects are qualitative only — no numeric coefficients. Use calibration experiments to quantify for your setup.

    Offline: Runs entirely in your browser. Data stored in localStorage. Nothing sent to any server.

    Add to Home Screen: In Safari, tap share (⬆) → "Add to Home Screen" for standalone app experience.

    Calibration Protocol

    Exp 1 — Baseline: Brew 3× with standard recipe. Compare predicted vs measured TDS. Offset > 0.10% → adjust constants.

    Exp 2 — Grind sweep: Same recipe at 3 grind settings. Validates EY-vs-grind curve.

    Exp 3 — Pour count: 3× continuous vs 3× pulse (3-pour). Keep pattern constant. |ΔTDS| > 0.05% = real effect.

    Exp 4 — Pour pattern: 3× centre vs 3× spiral. Keep count constant. Repeat on flat-bottom to check bypass.
