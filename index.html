ℹ️ About

Brew Coach v0.4.5

Physics-informed pour-over extraction model. Two-phase kinetics with d²-scaled Fickian diffusion (Moroney et al. 2015/2019; Wang & Lim 2021) and Arrhenius temperature correction (Ea = 16 kJ/mol).

What's new in v0.4.5

Core model refactor (peer-reviewed):
• τ_slow now scales with d² (Fickian diffusion), replacing linear d
• Temperature enters kinetics via Arrhenius, replacing additive offset
• D_eff = 7.685×10⁻¹¹ m²/s (fitted to baseline at 600 µm / 88°C / 124 s / TDS 1.36%)
• EUI renamed to "Brew Confidence Score" (heuristic, not measured uniformity)
• Predictions show ±σ uncertainty band
• MAX_EXTRACTABLE re-tagged CONVENTION-SCA (Lockhart 1957)
• F_s re-tagged GRINDER-SPECIFIC-DEFAULT (C40)
• Geometry bias re-tagged EMPIRICAL-FIT-USER-SPECIFIC
• RETAINED_RATIO re-tagged HEURISTIC-DEFAULT
• LRR measurement in back-calculator (weigh spent bed)
• New brew-log fields: pour height, pour rate, jet integrity, spent bed mass
• Frost et al. 2023 cited on SCA chart
• Pour-technique effects remain qualitative only

Calibration Protocol

Exp 1 — Baseline (DONE): 3 brews. Model matches within −0.02% TDS. D_eff fitted.
Exp 2 — Grind sweep (NEXT): 17 clicks & 23 clicks × 3 replicates. Validates d² slope. Falsification: measured slope outside −0.030 ± 0.015 %TDS/click.
Exp 3 — Pour count: 3× continuous vs 3× pulse. |ΔTDS| > 0.05% = real effect.
Exp 4 — Pour pattern: 3× centre vs 3× spiral. Repeat on flat-bottom.
Exp 9 — Pour height (NEW): 3 heights × 3 replicates. Validates Park et al. 2025.
Protocol: Weigh spent bed after each brew for LRR measurement. Record sample temp.
