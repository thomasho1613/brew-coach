ℹ️ About

Brew Coach v0.4.6

Physics-informed pour-over extraction model. Two-phase kinetics with d²-scaled Fickian diffusion (Moroney et al. 2015/2019; Wang & Lim 2021) and Arrhenius temperature correction (Ea = 16 kJ/mol).

What's new in v0.4.6

Shell-corrected extraction model (Exp 2 validated):
• Accessible-fraction correction: maxEY = 0.30 × accFrac × rm. Based on Gagné 2019 shell model.
• PENETRATION_DEPTH (λ) = 200 µm (fitted to Exp 2 grind sweep).
• D_eff refitted: 1.05×10⁻¹⁰ m²/s (was 7.685×10⁻¹¹). Fitted to Exp 1 + Exp 2 (5 data points).
• RETAINED_RATIO: 2.3 mL/g (measured, was 2.0 heuristic).
• All Exp 2 residuals ≤ ±0.03 % TDS.

What was new in v0.4.5

Core model refactor (peer-reviewed):
• τ_slow now scales with d² (Fickian diffusion), replacing linear d
• Temperature enters kinetics via Arrhenius, replacing additive offset
• D_eff = 7.685×10⁻¹¹ m²/s (fitted to baseline at 600 µm / 88°C / 124 s / TDS 1.36%)
• EUI renamed to "Brew Confidence Score" (heuristic, not measured uniformity)
• Predictions show ±σ uncertainty band
• Pour-technique effects remain qualitative only

Calibration Protocol

Exp 1 — Baseline (DONE): 3 brews at 20 g/300 g, 20 clicks, 88 °C. Mean TDS 1.36 %. D_eff fitted.
Exp 2 — Grind sweep (DONE): 12 brews at 15 g/225 g. Clicks 17/20/23/26 × 3 replicates. Slope −0.040 %TDS/click. λ and D_eff refitted. Residuals ≤ ±0.03 % TDS.
Exp 3 — Pour count (NEXT): 3× continuous vs 3× pulse. |ΔTDS| > 0.05% = real effect.
Exp 4 — Pour pattern: 3× centre vs 3× spiral. Repeat on flat-bottom.
Exp 9 — Pour height: 3 heights × 3 replicates. Validates Park et al. 2025.
Protocol: Weigh spent bed after each brew for LRR measurement. Record sample temp.
